# Cooking style

We cook from raw ingredients: sauces, dressings, marinades from scratch.
Prefer fresh, seasonal produce. Open to most cuisines.

## Preferences

- Healthy, bright, acidic food
- Moderately spicy (kids tolerate some heat but nothing punishing)
- Not afraid of effort on weeknights; we cook properly, not just assemble

## We avoid

- Pre-made sauces and dressings
- Processed convenience food
