# Sourcing

Not everything comes from Willys. Split by source to keep quality high.

## Willys (via this app)

- Everyday proteins: mince, chicken, sausage, cured meats
- Dairy, eggs, bread
- Vegetables, fruit
- Pantry staples, drinks, snacks

## Butcher (buy in person)

- Premium cuts: entrecote, tenderloin, lamb
- Special occasion roasts

## Fishmonger (buy in person)

- All fish and seafood — never from Willys
- Pick day-of based on what's fresh

When a dinner needs a butcher or fishmonger item, only the sides go into the
Willys cart. The recipe should note what to pick up elsewhere.
