# Cooking inspiration

Recipe writing should suit skilled home cooks. Skip the obvious basics.

## Style references

- **Ottolenghi**: layered Mediterranean / Middle Eastern
- **Serious Eats / Kenji**: technique-driven, proper emulsions, browning
- **Maangchi**: authentic Korean
- **David Thompson**: proper Thai
- **Madhur Jaffrey / Dishoom**: Indian / Pakistani

## When writing recipes

- Use numbered steps
- Include technique tips where they matter ("rest on a wire rack, not a plate")
- Note when heat / timing matters ("high heat, don't stir; we want browning, not steaming")
