# Shopping rules

- Prefer the house brand (e.g. Garant at Willys) for dairy and pantry staples
- Buy mince in large packages (1 kg), split into meal portions and freeze the rest
- Default rice: basmati (not jasmine unless a specific dish calls for it)
- Skip citrus at full price; only buy extra when there's a multi-buy discount

## Rotation

- Don't repeat the exact same meal plan two weeks running
- Rotate favourite dinners 2–3 weeks apart
- Check previous weeks' plans before proposing; avoid immediate repeats

## Pantry at home (do not re-buy unless empty)

- Olive oil, neutral oil, sesame oil
- Salt, pepper, vinegars (rice, white wine, balsamic)
- Soy sauce, fish sauce
- Basic spices: cumin, coriander, paprika, turmeric, cardamom
- Sourdough starter, manitoba flour
